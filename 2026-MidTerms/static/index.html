2026 MIDTERMS: DATA IS POWER.

THE VOTE.

V1 tracks every major roll call in the House and Senate. Don't listen to what they say on the news; look at how they voted on the floor. This is the only record that matters when you head to the polls.

THE ALIGNMENT.

This metric provides an objective view of legislative alignment with the current administration, offering a transparent record for voters across the political spectrum to assess consistency and independence.

THE MONEY.

Through our integrated OpenSecrets portals, you can see who is funding your representatives in real-time. Whether it's Big Oil, the Gun Lobby, or Big Pharma, you deserve to know whose interests are being represented before the 2026 election.

THE REACH.

Politics isn't a spectator sport. Use the built-in contact tools to call or email your reps directly. A single phone call from a constituent carries more weight than a thousand tweets. Make your voice heard before the cycle ends.
